## Recommended preparation

- Use PNG with transparent backgrounds for logos.
- Keep logo files roughly square; 256–512 px is sufficient.
- Keep card artwork near a standard 1.586:1 card ratio.
- Optimize files before copying them into the repository.
- Do not include personal card numbers, names, CVV values, or expiry dates.
